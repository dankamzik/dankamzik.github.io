title:  "Gallows"
date:   2018-04-28 19:38:00 -0400
categories: poetry
---
Amber glow
gives me hope
Dancing in the flames
Man out of time, at end of rope
Holds his head up high
He's brave
A lonely crow caws
Not knowing the cause
A long, shrill wave
Nails in boards squeak
And swinging ropes creak
Dark eyes
Dark wings
Dark beak
