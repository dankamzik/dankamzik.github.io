title:  "Sarsaparilla Float"
date:   2018-04-28 19:38:00 -0400
categories: poetry
---
Floating

In a river

Brown

And sweet

Effervescent

With the scent

Of vanilla

and

Graveyard dirt

Cold stone

Slowly warmed

By a rising sun

Walking barefoot

and being

all the richer for it

Two children

Fingers sticky with

Sarsaparilla
